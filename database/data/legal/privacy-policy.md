## 1. Commitment to Privacy

The Company respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and applicable regulations.

For purposes of this Privacy Policy:

- **"Personal Data"** shall have the meaning assigned under the Data Protection Act, 2019; and
- **"Processing"** includes collection, recording, organization, storage, adaptation, retrieval, disclosure, transfer, use, deletion or destruction of personal data.

## 2. Information We Collect

- **Personal Information** - Name, National ID or passport number, Email address, Telephone number, Postal and physical address.
- **Business Information** - Company name, Business registration details, Tax information, Authorized representatives, Procurement contacts.
- **Service Information** - Site addresses, Project specifications, Equipment records, Installation reports, Maintenance records, Warranty records.
- **Technical Information** - IP address, Browser information, Device information, Website usage information.
- **Monitoring Information** - Where applicable: Equipment performance data, Temperature monitoring data, Fault reports, Remote diagnostics information.

## 3. Purpose of Processing

We process information to provide services, process transactions, manage contracts, deliver products, schedule maintenance, respond to inquiries, administer warranties, improve services, conduct quality assurance, comply with legal obligations and protect our legitimate business interests.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

The Company processes personal data only where it has a lawful basis to do so under applicable data protection laws. The legal basis relied upon will depend on the nature of the personal data being processed and the purpose for which it is processed.

The Company may process personal data on one or more of the following legal bases:

- The data subject has provided consent for the processing of his or her personal data for one or more specific purposes;
- The processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is a party or in order to take steps at the request of the data subject prior to entering into a contract;
- The processing is necessary for compliance with a legal or regulatory obligation to which the Company is subject;
- The processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by the Company or a third party, provided that such interests are not overridden by the rights and freedoms of the data subject;
- The processing is necessary to protect the vital interests of the data subject or another natural person; or
- Any other lawful basis permitted under applicable data protection laws.

Where processing is based on consent, the data subject may withdraw such consent at any time, provided that the withdrawal shall not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before such withdrawal.
